How to Choose the right Furniture ?

Buying Guide for Sofa

STEP 1: YOU WILL NEED TO CONSIDER THE ATTRIBUTES OF A SOFA

The Kind Of A Sit You Require

A higher seating height in a Sofa, provides the ease of getting up, whereas a lower seating is more casual and relaxed. Therefore, before making a decision, do keep in mind who is going to be using the Sofa - are they tall, short or older people? The seating height of our Sofas vary in sit – from the very low ones having a seating height of 8 inches to the taller variety going up to 24 inches. Though standard seating height is 18 inches.

The Width You Need

The width of a Sofa plays an important criteria in decision making too. You’ll need to measure the available space in your lounge to decide whether it is the 3, 2, 1 seater or large sectionals that your room needs.

STEP 2: WEIGH IN THE PROS AND CONS FOR DIFFERENT SEATING TYPES

For L-shaped sofa Just make sure that is it the RHS (right hand side ) or LHS (left hand side ) Sofa Sectional you require, so figure out where exactly are you going to place it and decide which L shape part of your home you plan to utilize

For Recliners,Make sure there is enough room at the back and front of the Sofa, so either the manually-operated or electrical mechanism allows it open up completely and recline backwards or the front, giving you ample space to stretch your feet.

STEP 3: THINK THROUGH THE FOLLOWING POINTERS

1.Are there little ones at home?
Then a dark toned sofa would be wiser choice.
2.What’s the weather like where you live?
For an all-year humid weather like Mumbai, leather may not be a great idea, whereas for a blessed weather like Bengaluru, it works just fine!
3.What’s your home style?
If you have a laidback ambience, a recliner would be a good fit.
4.What is your room’s colour scheme like?
If bright and chirpy is your thing, maybe a contrasting neutral piece would be right for you.
